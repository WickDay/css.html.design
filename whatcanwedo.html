What we can do to help?

There are a few basic things you can do at home to be more eco-friendly.

It is important to make sure your garbage is separated so the correct facilities receive the right garbage since certain trash requires different equipment.

Try to save electricity by using clean reusable energy like solar power or wind power or even switching more energy efficient lightbulbs would help.

Reduce paper consumption. Paper will degrade over several recycling processes and would eventually be unusable after a few recycling rounds.
